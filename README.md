Simple userscript for iOS/Mobile Safari

1: You will need a userscript manager on your iPhone. (Userscripts or Stay etc.)

2: Head to: https://greasyfork.org/en/scripts/560550-optitranslate 

3: One installed it will automatically detect websites that are not in your native language and a will offer to translate via; 
> Google Translate
> Yandex Translate
> Bing Translate

4: Simple, lightweight and free
